Who we are

Scorpio Services Inc is a reliable trucking and logistics company operating since July 2020. We specialize in 53’ Dry Van trailers with swing doors and provide safe, efficient freight transportation across the U.S.

We work with top national brokers and Amazon, offering drop & hook services, a dedicated warehouse, and a strategically located yard in Wheeling, IL.

Our focus is simple — on-time delivery, clear communication, and long-term partnerships.

Freight types: all general freight except bales
